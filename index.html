Privacy Policy - Ninjagrid

Effective date: May 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ninjagrid (com.rpd33studio.ninjagrid), published by RPD33 Studio, handles data.

1. Data Collection

Ninjagrid does not require account creation and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number from inside the app experience.

The app uses third-party services, including Google AdMob, which may process technical data such as device identifiers, diagnostics, and ad interaction data to provide and measure ads.

2. How Data Is Used

Processed data may be used to:

  • Deliver ads and measure ad performance.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Maintain app stability and service reliability.

3. Data Sharing

Data may be shared with service providers (for example, Google AdMob) only as needed to provide app and advertising functionality.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Ninjagrid stores gameplay progress locally on your device. You can remove local app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

5. Children

Ninjagrid is not specifically directed to children unless explicitly stated in the Google Play listing and target audience settings.

6. Security

We use reasonable measures and rely on platform and service-provider protections to safeguard data processed in connection with the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
